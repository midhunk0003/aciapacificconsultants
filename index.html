Indoor Air Quality Assessment

At ASIA PACIFIC CONSULTANCY SOLUTIONS Facility Solutions, we offer comprehensive Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) Assessment services to ensure a healthy and productive indoor environment. Our IAQ testing is conducted in accordance with ASHRAE standards to accurately evaluate air quality and identify potential contaminants that may impact occupant health and productivity. HVAC Retrofit

At ASIA PACIFIC CONSULTANCY SOLUTIONS Facility Solutions, our HVAC Retrofit services are designed to enhance the efficiency and longevity of your HVAC systems. We offer a range of solutions including Condenser Coil Replacement to boost cooling efficiency, Chiller Evaporator Tube Replacement for optimal performance, and Compressor Overhauling to maximize reliability and reduce energy consumption. Non-Destructive Testing

ASIA PACIFIC CONSULTANCY SOLUTIONS provides comprehensive Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services to ensure the integrity of your materials and structures. Our Eddy Current Test detects surface and subsurface flaws in conductive materials, while the Ultrasonic Thickness Test measures material thickness and identifies internal defects. Radiography Testing visualizes internal structures using X-rays or gamma rays to uncover hidden flaws. Our Dye Penetration Test reveals surface-breaking defects with high sensitivity, and Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI) identifies discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials. We also offer detailed Visual Inspection to detect surface imperfections and anomalies.

Condition Monitoring

Our Condition Monitoring services at ASIA PACIFIC CONSULTANCY SOLUTIONS are essential for maintaining optimal performance and preventing equipment failures. We conduct Vibration Analysis to diagnose mechanical vibrations, ensuring reliability. Laser Alignment services guarantee precise alignment of rotating machinery, while Dynamic Balancing corrects imbalances to enhance stability. Thermography detects thermal anomalies to prevent overheating, and Oil Analysis assesses lubricant health to predict machinery breakdowns. Acoustic Emission Testing listens for high-frequency sounds to identify structural defects, and Motor Current Analysis helps detect faults early. Bearing Condition Monitoring and Infrared Thermography further support proactive maintenance and extend equipment life.
